Matter QR Tool

Decode a Matter QR image, inspect the extracted payload, and download a generated SVG. The core decode flow stays local in your browser unless you explicitly run the optional CSA DCL lookup.

Default use stays local in your browser: uploaded images, raw QR contents, setup PINs, and pairing codes are not sent anywhere. If you click the optional live CSA DCL lookup, only the extracted vendor and product IDs are sent through this site to the official CSA DCL service.
